About me

I'm a full stack web developer and Linux systems administrator residing in Minneapolis, MN.

My journey as a web developer started with building a simple WordPress website for my landscaping services business, giving me a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and managed hosting with cPanel.

As my skills evolved, so to did my passion for web development. I soon felt constrained by the limitations of managed hosting, and I began exploring cloud computing platforms. After months of self-paced learning, I was able to get a grasp of cloud computing fundamentals, and began publishing tutorials and offering free support to other WordPress developers getting started in the cloud.

As I became increasingly comfortable with Linux systems administration, I began offering paid services for individuals and small business looking to move their applications to the cloud. This growing passion for web development led me to enroll in the full stack web development program at the University of Minnesota, where I was able to build my skills with Javascript, databases, and essential web development frameworks.

Having recently graduated from the full-stack web development program, I am currently seeking a full-time position as a Full-Stack Developer in a collaborative, immersive environment.
